The `commerce.get_matrix` snippet is used to output a [product matrix](../Product_Catalog/Product_Matrix)

Added in 0.12.

## Usage

```` html
[[!commerce.get_matrix? &matrix=`123`]]
````

## Properties

- `&matrix`: the ID of the matrix to output.
